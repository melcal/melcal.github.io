About eChats

Technology consistently changes the way we think about the world at a pace never seen before. Created by Kellogg students for Kellogg students, the Kellogg eChats series provides MBA candidates with an opportunity to meet leading thought leaders. The monthly series affords students an opportunity to learn from these leaders on topics such as wearables, bitcoin, and other such topics that are changing the way we interact with technology and pose their own questions.
